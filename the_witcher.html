The aim of the project

The purpose of this project, is to train us on HTML with CSS and the rules of SEO. We learned the significance of organizing our ideas and setting up our schedule in order to not be overhelmed by the two weeks we had to create this site that we're proud of.

About The Witcher III : Wild Hunt

The Witcher III : Wild Hunt is a game developped by CD PROJEKT: RED, a well-known polish studio in 2015. We choose this game because we love the universe of the saga, and the gameplay of this rewareded RPG open world is a pleasure to play. The artistic direction of the game is wonderful and landscapes are even more. When you launch the game, you'll be took in an incredible environement and a beautiful journey.
