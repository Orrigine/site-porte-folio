A dark, fantastic and breathtaking saga

Incarnate Geralt of Rivia, the monsters killer and accept the most important contract of your life : find the child of the Prophety, a human weapon who could change the world.

The great story of the Witcher

Geralt of Rivia is a legendary witcher of the Wolf's School of witcher active throughout the 13th century. He became Ciri's adoptive father by invoquing the Law of Suprise : an old tradition of giving to your benefactor what you don't expect to own. And this happend at a wedding ceremony.

The world is waiting you

Play one of the most rewarded RPG of all time in your house or in another place and explore a world torned up by monsters and wars. Prepare yourself for wonderful landscapes, charismatic characters, and thrilling aventures.

Influence of the game

The game by its narrative diversity in the side quests create a unique and personnaly ambiance which valued a lot of compliments and rewards. And a particular way of seeing a fight, which will invite you to turn things in your head in order to prepare your conflict.

A well-known universe

Netflix adapted the original story from the books into a series with Henry Cavill who incarnate Geralt of Rivia. The series hyped the witcher's fans and people who didn't know The Witcher. The show is a success. Netflix made an animation film, Nightmare of the Wolf, which relate the story of Vesemir.
